Summary

*Ramayan: The Prince* is an adaptation of the ancient Indian epic Ramayana, which follows the journey of Prince Rama. The story centers on his quest to rescue his wife Sita from the demon king Ravana, embodying themes of duty, honor, and devotion. Through trials, battles, and divine interventions, the tale showcases the virtues of sacrifice, courage, and loyalty.

Actors

The main characters include Rama, portrayed by Arun Govil; Sita, portrayed by Deepika Chikhalia; Lakshmana by Sunil Lahri; and Ravana, portrayed by Arvind Trivedi. The cast is celebrated for bringing these legendary figures to life with sincerity and devotion.
